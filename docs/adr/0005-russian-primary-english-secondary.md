# Russian primary, English secondary

In v1, the product supports Russian as the primary language and English as the secondary language. This affects app UI, Client Reminder text, Profile Share pages, Share Images, and date/time presentation, so localization is treated as part of the initial product shape rather than a later retrofit. Stylist language drives the app UI, while Client language drives Client Reminders and Profile Shares and defaults from the Stylist's current language when the Client is created.
